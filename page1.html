Putt Your Money Where Your Mouth Is

  • "Putt Your Money Where Your Mouth" Is is a golf-based 3D physics puzzle game made in Unity
  • The game was entirely made by Chris Orlassino, with assets coming from various sources
  • In gameplay, the player is limited by their budget and their number of swings
  • With their budget, a player may purchase several different geometries:
  • The player uses these geometries to provide desirable angles to get efficient strokes
  • In "build mode", the player edits the geometry by changing the shape, size, and angle:
  • In "play mode", the player strikes their golf ball using the course they just built
